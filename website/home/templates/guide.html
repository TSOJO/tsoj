Criteria for contributor
Contributors are allowed to create, edit, delete problems and view non-public problems. At least one of the following must be satisfied for one to be a contributor:
  1. Suggested by a teacher.
  2. Is a secretary of Programming Society.
  3. Suggested by the majority of the secretaries of Programming Society.
